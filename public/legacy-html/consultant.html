Prof. Dr. Hosne Ara Baby ( Consultant)

Prof. Dr. Hosne Ara Baby is an expert and competent OB/GYN and Fertility specialist. She completed her graduation in medical science in 1985 from SSMC and Fellowship in OB/GYN from BCPS in 1994. She did government service for 20 years and worked as resident surgeon and consultant in different govt. Medical College Hospital. In 2006 she joined Labaid specialized hospital as senior consultant and worked there for 2 years. In 2008 she joined Enam medical college as Associate professor and upgraded as Professor in 2009. Now she is working as professor in Anwer Khan Modern Medical College. She received special training on laparoscopic surgery and regularly practicing this surgery. She received extensive training on infertility management and Assisted Reproductive Technology under direct guidance of Dr. Rashida Begum who is the founder of ICRC (Infertility Care & Research Centre). In addition she also received training from IRM (Institute of Reproductive Medicine), India under supervision of Prof, B N Chakravarty. She joined ICRC and is doing regular consultancy as a fertility specialist. She also offers consultations to the patients of Sylhet on Friday at IBNSINA Hospital there.


I love being here – it’s the most magical place - and we have the greatest patients in Bangladesh. Dr. Rashida Begum set the example – she includes us in her ICRC family, and we include our patients in the same circle of love and support. I feel proud and lucky to be here.” Dr. Hosne Ara Baby

 

 
Dr. Maruf Siddiqui ( Consultant)

Dr. Maruf Siddiqui is a young energetic OB/GYN and infertility specialist with a high level of confidence, dynamism and dedication in infertility management. He graduated from Dhaka Medical College in 1998 and completed Fellowship in Obstetrics & Gynaecology (FCPS) in 2006. He worked as Indoor Medical Officer, Assistant Registrar, Registrar, Resident Surgeon and Consultant in different Medical Colleges in Bangladesh for 10 years. Now he is working as Assistant Professor in Anower Khan Modern Medical College. For his paper on ovulation induction using letrozole in clomiphene resistant PCOS patients; he was awarded the ‘Young Gynaecologist Award (YGA)’ in 2009 by Asia Oceania Federation of Obstetrics & Gynaecology (AOFOG). He received extensive training on ‘Infertility & Assisted Reproductive Technology Laboratory’ at the Siriraj IVF, Mahidol University, Thailand in 2009. He has got 10 papers published in reputed National & International Journals and has presented many papers on International Conferences. He is one of the contributors of the book ‘Aromatase Inhibitors: Types, Mode of Action & Indications’ Published by Nova Science Publishers, NY, USA. Dr.Siddiqui joined ICRC as a Consultant infertility specialist. He also offers consultations to the patients of Chittagong on Fridays at Chevron & CSCR regularly.
