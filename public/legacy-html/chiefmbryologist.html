Dr. Ehsan Quadir ( Chief Embryologist)

 

Dr. Ehsan Quadir graduated in 1980 from Dhaka Medical College and completed Diploma in Child Health from The University of Dhaka in 1995. In spite of specilaity in different field he acquired the knowledge and skills of embryology to establish this centre. He got extensive training on embryology from India. He is dedicated in lab management and lab work. He has more than 20 national and international publications.
