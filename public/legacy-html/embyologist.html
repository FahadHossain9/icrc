Dr. Sahina Begum Santa ( Embyologist)


Dr. Sahina completed her undergraduate studies from Dhaka Medical College in 2003. Since then she has been working in ICRC with devotion and fascination. As a result she became an embryologist with state of the art perfection. She got her advanced training from KK IVF and Womens Hospital Singapore. She performed more than thousand cases of IVF /ICSI and a number of embryo freezing and thawing. She has completed her Fellowship in OB/GYN from BCPS in January 2011.

 

“I truly love what I do. I have always been fascinated by the science of reproduction. I really enjoy interacting with patients, talking with them, and helping them understand the problem and solution exactly what we do in the lab. I understand very well the feelings and emotions that patients are dealing with. I am fortunate to have the opportunity to work with Dr. Rashida Begum who made me an expert in this field and I look forward to our continued success." -
Dr. Sahina Begum

 

 
Dr. Farzana Khan ( Embryologist)


Dr. Farzana Khan completed her undergraduate studies from Uttara Women Medical College in 2001. She worked as medical officer in private hospitals and joined as medical officer in ICRC in 2004. She was working as IVF/ICSI co-ordinator. Due to her enthusiasm she acquired all skills of ART like stimulation for IVF/ICSI, monitoring, ovum pickup, all laboratory works including embryo freezing, embryo transfer etc. She got training in ART from Soong Poong Women’s Clinic, South Korea in 2007. Her journey in the management of infertility is parallel to the journey of Dr. Rashida Begum like from reception to discharge of the patient. She is the nucleus of ICRC.


I am extremely happy to be a part of ICRC and proud to say that I have a job that I love, and I get to see miracles happen every day. I am very committed and dedicated in helping you actualize your hopes and dreams of starting or adding to your family. It is my goal to establish and achieve outstanding success rates for ICRC -
Dr. Farzana Khan
